About Me:

Hey there! I'm Tapas, a proud native of the vibrant city of Godhra, nestled in the heart of Gujarat. Ever since I can remember, I've been fascinated by the enchanting world of computers. My journey into the realm of coding began in the 6th standard when I dabbled with HTML, and by the 8th or 9th standard, I was already experimenting with CSS and JavaScript. Fast forward to after my 10th grade, I made the bold decision to pursue a diploma in Computer Engineering. But before diving into college life, I took a detour to master C language in computer classes.

Oh, and did I mention my rendezvous with cybersecurity? Yup, I turned my low-end PC into a playground for Kali Linux, navigating the depths of Linux OS through self-taught lessons and endless YouTube binges. From HTML to hacking, I'm on a quest to unravel the mysteries of tech with a dash of humor and a sprinkle of curiosity!
